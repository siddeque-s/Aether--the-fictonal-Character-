Story Of Aether


In the forgotten lands of Valthor — a realm cloaked in mist and myth — Aethor was born under a celestial alignment known as the "Forgemoon." From that moment, he was marked by fate. Gifted with the rare power of Aetherforge, Aethor could conjure, control, and even reshape weapons from memory, myth, or imagination. Swords forged in dragonfire, axes carved from stardust, bows that never missed — they all answered his call.

As he grew, so did his power — and with it, the fear of kings and empires. Once a champion to many, Aethor was betrayed by those who once praised him, condemned as a threat too great to rule beside. Hunted and exiled, he vanished into the world's darkest corners.

But he did not fall.

Now, he roams the war-torn realms as a mythic nomad, driven by a new purpose: to track down and seal cursed weapons before they fall into corrupt hands. Bound to the Arsenal Core — a sentient relic forged from the remnants of gods — Aethor can feel the pull of ancient blades echoing across dimensions.

Though thousands of weapons obey his command, he is a servant to none. Every scar on his armor tells a story, every weapon he bears holds a soul, and every enemy he faces learns the truth too late: Aethor is the master of all blades — bound by none.

Allies

Rynneth, The Ember-Eyed Ranger

Silent Watcher of the Wildwoods

Swift as the wind and silent as a shadow, Rynneth's golden eyes see what others cannot. Trained by an ancient forest tribe, he weaves between trees like a ghost, loosing arrows infused with ancient fire. His bond with Aethor stems from a shared hatred of corrupted relics, often scouting and eliminating threats before they even reach the party.

Vaelira, The Runechant Sorceress

Keeper of Forbidden Glyphs

With arcane runes spinning around her like stars, Vaelira channels forgotten magics to bend reality. Once a royal scholar, she abandoned the kingdom after discovering truths too dangerous to remain hidden. Now, she aids Aethor with powerful wards and explosive spells, decoding ancient weapon seals that even the Arsenal Core cannot translate alone.

Ser Thalor, The Eternal Crown

Warden of the Sacred Flame

Towering in golden armor and crowned with arcane emeralds, Ser Thalor is a living relic of the old world — a knight-king who never fell. Bound by oath to the ancient kingdom of Valthor, he rose again through divine flame to uphold forgotten vows. Silent and unwavering, Thalor now fights beside Aethor, his flaming blade forged to smite corrupted kings and shattered empires alike.

Enemies

Grulmar, The Ironfang Warlord

Brute of the Blighted Horde

A monstrous bugbear forged in endless battles, Grulmar is feared for his ruthless tactics and colossal strength. Armed with a jagged cleaver and impenetrable hide, he commands armies of raiders and warbeasts. His sole desire is to crush Aethor and seize the Arsenal Core to forge a weapon worthy of gods.

Morvak, The Obsidian Juggernaut

Breaker of Oaths

Encased in spiked gold-black armor, Morvak once swore loyalty to Valthor, but betrayed the realm for power. Now a dark champion of ruin, he crushes cities underfoot. His gauntlets are said to shatter magic barriers, and his hatred for Aethor is fueled by an ancient rivalry born in fire and betrayal.

Skulroth, The Reaping Shadow

Wielder of the Abyssal Blade

A lich-knight forged in undeath, Skulroth commands legions of wraiths and fallen souls. His crimson blade drains life with every strike. Once a fallen king cursed by his own sword, he now hunts Aethor to reclaim the Arsenal Core and plunge all realms into eternal dusk.
